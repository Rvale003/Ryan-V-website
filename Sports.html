Sports

I love basketball & football, they were my favorite sports to play and watch growing up, and still are my favorite sports now.

Basketball(NBA)

Russell Westbrook

Kevin Durant

While I do not have a favorite basketball team anymore, my favorite players to watch and follow are Russell Westbrook and Kevin Durant.
I am a bigger fan of Russell Westbrook, I enjoy the passion and energy he brings to his team, my favorite part of his game is the explosiveness he has when attacking the rim.

As a basketball fan I love listening to stories about the game and players talking about the game of basketball in general, as well as their off the court life.
There is one podcast that I absolutely love called "All The Smoke" hosted by former players Matt Barnes & Stephen Jackson, check it out on apple podcasts All The Smoke

Football(NFL)

Peyton Manning(left) & Marvin Harrison(right)

As for football my favorite team is the Indianapolis Colts, I have been a fan of Peyton Manning & Marvin Harrison ever since I started watching the game as a kid and stuck with the Colts ever since.
Peyton Manning is one of the best quarterbacks I have seen play football.
My favorite thing about him was the way he was able to recognize defensive schemes and change plays at the line of scrimmage to beat those schemes.
With Marvin Harrison, he is one of my favorite wide recievers because of his route running ability and his ability to catch almost anything thrown his way.
